Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It's creamy, savory, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 4 oz pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  2. In a skillet, cook pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta and garlic. Remove from heat, then quickly pour in the egg and cheese mixture, tossing everything together until the spaghetti is coated.
  5. If the sauce is too thick, add reserved pasta water gradually until desired consistency is reached. Serve immediately.
